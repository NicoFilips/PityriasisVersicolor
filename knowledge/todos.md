## Symptom Map
- **Worst area:** Chest, especially under the chest
- **Moderate:** Back (more intense than arms, some flaking), neck, shoulders
- **Mild:** Upper arms, groin area, scalp (barely noticeable, very little flaking)
- **Itch:** Present, improves as symptoms improve
- **Pattern:** Circular rashes, mostly brown/red, intensify with carbs/alcohol
- **Skin texture:** Flakes/peels, very dry, sometimes has cracks
- **Sweat:** Intensifies redness
