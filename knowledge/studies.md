### Community Claim: "Protein Deficiency" Theory (Reddit, anecdotal)

Source: Reddit user claiming 30 years of TV experience. **Not a doctor — anecdotal.**

**Claim:** Our bodies have a "protein deficiency" that prevents us from fighting Malassezia spores naturally. This was allegedly told to them by a doctor decades ago.

**Assessment:**
- The term "protein deficiency" is vague — could refer to a specific immune protein/enzyme deficiency, or a misremembering of a more nuanced explanation
- There IS evidence that certain immune deficiencies (e.g., defects in Th17 response, antimicrobial peptides) may predispose people to chronic PV — this could be what the doctor meant
- Worth researching: Are there specific immune proteins or enzymes that are deficient in chronic PV sufferers?
- **TODO:** Look into studies on immune protein deficiencies and Malassezia susceptibility

**Prevalence claim (likely wrong):** Same user states PV affects "1 in 50,000." This contradicts medical literature — PV prevalence is estimated at 1-8% of the population in temperate climates, and up to 40-50% in tropical/humid regions. The 1 in 50,000 figure may refer to a severe or chronic form, or may simply be inaccurate.
